DUBAI MALLS

The Dubai Mall

  • Located in Downtown Dubai, near the Burj Khalifa
  • Largest mall in the world by area, featuring the Dubai Aquarium, Ice Rink, and a massive selection of luxury and high-street brands.
  • Features over 1,200 retail outlets, 2 department stores, and 220 gold stores
  • Includes more than 150 food and beverage outlets
  • Provides a unique blend of shopping, dining, entertainment, and cultural experiences
  • Major attractions:-
    1. Dubai Aquarium (one of the largest suspended aquariums in the world).
    2. Dubai Fountain (world's largest choreographed fountain system).
    3. VR Park (indoor virtual reality theme park).
    4. Dubai Dino (155-million-year-old dinosaur skeleton display).
    5. Fashion Avenue (luxury shopping area with designer brands).

Mall of the Emirates

  • Located in Al Barsha 1, Dubai, UAE
  • One of the largest shopping centers in Dubai, covering an area of approximately 2.4 million square feet
  • Features over 700 retail outlets, including luxury brands and international retailers
  • Offers a wide range of dining options, from fast food to fine dining
  • Provides a mix of shopping, entertainment, and leisure activities for visitors of all ages:-
    1. Ski Dubai (indoor ski resort)
    2. Magic Planet (indoor amusement park)
    3. VOX Cinemas (multiplex movie theater)
    4. VR Park (virtual reality entertainment center)

Dubai Marina Mall

  • Located on Sheikh Zayed Road, near Dubai Marina (also known as Dubai Marina District)
  • The mall has 140 stores, 21 dining options, and a children's play area
  • It spans over 390,000 square feet
  • Many tourists visit Dubai Marina Mall specifically because of its central location and proximity to various attractions in the city.
  • Nearby attractions:
    1. Dubai Marina Walk
    2. Dubai Eye (Dubai Ferris Wheel)
    3. Jumeirah Beach Residence (JBR)
    4. Palm Jumeirah
